Introduction to Golang

Why Golang?

Golang (or Go) is a statically typed, compiled programming language designed at Google. It focuses on simplicity, performance, and efficient concurrency.

What makes Go unique?

  • Built-in concurrency using goroutines and channels
  • Fast compilation and execution
  • Simple syntax, minimalistic design
  • Great for system programming, APIs, and microservices
